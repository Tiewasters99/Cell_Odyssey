From the Cellular to the Cosmic.

We build worlds within worlds. Odyssey Interactive is a new studio dedicated to the art of discovery through interactive entertainment.

Our Mission

At Odyssey Interactive, we believe that the greatest adventures are journeys into the unknown. Our mission is to create breathtaking, narrative-driven experiences that explore the unseen wonders of our universe—from the bustling ecosystems within a single cell to the vast, silent architecture of the cosmos.

Debut Voyage

Cell Odyssey

Our premier title invites you to pilot Cammie, a state-of-the-art nano-camera, on a mission of a lifetime. Navigate the treacherous, beautiful landscape of a human cell to capture the first-ever high-resolution images of the mysterious Golgi Apparatus. Danger, discovery, and stunning beauty await.

Future Worlds

The Cellular

Explore the fundamental building blocks of life. Uncover the intricate machinery and hidden dramas playing out in a world thriving just beneath our notice.

The Atomic

Venture beyond the molecular. Experience reality on a quantum scale, where the laws of physics become a strange and wondrous playground for new forms of storytelling.

The Galactic

Journey to the final frontier. Chart unknown star systems, encounter breathtaking cosmic phenomena, and unravel the ancient mysteries woven into the fabric of space-time.

The Temporal

Guide Algo, a geophysicist's sentient algorithm, on the ultimate journey: backward through time itself. Evade the cataclysms of dying epochs, witness the dawn of life, and endure the raw power of creation to discover the singular moment it all began.
